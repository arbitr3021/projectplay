Starting Up
IKEA is a multinational conglomerate founded in 1943 by Ingvar Kamprad. It initially began as a small mail-order business but eventually grew to become the world's largest furniture retailer in 2008.
The recognizable IKEA furniture has a distinctive style, blue buildings with yellow accents, and a complex corporate structure. Furthermore, the original IKEA catalogues are a well-known tool used by consumers.

Company Growth
IKEA has stores in over 50 countries and is known for its competitively-priced products, cost control, and continuous product development. It has become a very successful retailer due to these factors.

The IKEA catalogues succumbed to decreasing demand due to the COVID-19 pandemic in 2022 but that has not stopped stores from bouncing back from the economic impact the virus had on many businesses.
